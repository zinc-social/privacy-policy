Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General

Q: How do I turn off notifications?

A: Go to Profile > Settings > Notifications, there you can decide for what things you would like to receive notifications.

Q: How do I get friends?

A: We don't know, but in our app you can create relationships by importing existing contacts in Profile > Settings > Imported Contacts.

Bug Reporting
